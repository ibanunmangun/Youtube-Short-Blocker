# How to Install the YouTube Shorts Blocker

Follow these simple steps to install your "Custom Filter" in your Chrome browser:

1.  **Open Chrome Extensions:**
    - Type `chrome://extensions/` in your browser's address bar and press **Enter**.
2.  **Turn on Developer Mode:**
    - In the top-right corner of the Extensions page, find the switch for **Developer mode** and turn it **ON**.
3.  **Load the Extension:**
    - Click the **Load unpacked** button that appears in the top-left.
    - Browse to the folder where you saved this project: `C:\Users\chron\OneDrive\Documents\Research\CLI\Youtube-short-block\src`.
    - Select the `src` folder and click **Select Folder**.
4.  **Confirm Installation:**
    - You should now see "YouTube Shorts Blocker" in your list of extensions.
5.  **Test It Out:**
    - Open [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com) and verify that the "Shorts" sections are gone!
6.  **Using Focus Mode:**
    - Click on the extension's icon to open the popup.
    - Toggle "Focus Mode" on to hide distracting recommendation sidebars and related videos while you watch.
    - Unlike "Block Shorts," this focus on removing the distractions *around* the video you are watching.
